  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data ("Right to be Forgotten").
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Request limitation of how we process your data.
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or Switzerland, we process your personal data in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Our lawful bases for processing include:
