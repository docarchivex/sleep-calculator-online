Our Mission

At Sleep Calculator Tools, we believe that great days start with great nights. Our mission is to make sleep science accessible to everyone — completely free, forever. We built this tool because we saw a simple but widespread problem: millions of people wake up feeling tired, not because they don't sleep enough, but because they wake up at the wrong time in their sleep cycle.

Our sleep calculator solves this by using the scientifically established 90-minute sleep cycle model to find the optimal times to go to bed or set your alarm. The result? You wake up between sleep cycles — when your body is naturally lightest in sleep — feeling refreshed, alert, and ready for the day.

The Science Behind Our Calculator

Our calculations are based on well-established sleep research:

  • 90-Minute Sleep Cycle Model: Research shows that human sleep follows approximately 90-minute cycles, each progressing through light sleep (Stages 1-2), deep sleep (Stage 3), and REM sleep.
  • Sleep Latency: Studies indicate the average healthy adult takes about 10-20 minutes to fall asleep. We use a 14-minute average based on published research.
  • Cycle-Aligned Waking: Sleep science demonstrates that waking at the end of a cycle (during light sleep) results in significantly less grogginess than waking mid-cycle (during deep sleep).
  • Age-Based Recommendations: Our sleep duration guidelines follow the National Sleep Foundation's recommendations, which are based on extensive systematic reviews.
